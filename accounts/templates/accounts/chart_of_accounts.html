Account Categories
  • Assets: Resources owned by the business (Cash, Inventory, etc.)
  • Liabilities: Amounts owed to others (Accounts Payable, Loans, etc.)
  • Equity: Owner's interest in the business (Capital, Retained Earnings)
  • Revenue: Income from business activities (Sales, Service Revenue)
  • Expenses: Costs incurred in running the business (Rent, Utilities, etc.)
Features
  • All amounts are displayed in BDT (Bangladesh Taka)
  • Positive balances are shown in green, negative in red
  • Each account shows its code, type, and current balance
  • Accounts are organized by categories for easy navigation
  • Add new accounts and categories using the forms above
